PROGRAMMING AND DATA SCIENCE

IIT Madras, India’s top technical institute, welcomes you to the world’s first BSc Degree program in Programming and Data Science.

For the first time, you can work towards an undergraduate degree / diploma from an IIT regardless of your age or location, and with a wide range of academic backgrounds.

Qualifier course content release date for September Qualifier batch is 6th Sep 2021.

Applications open for September 2021 Batch

2020 and 2021 batch Std XII Students can apply now - Eligibility conditions (Std XII pass and college admission) need to be fulfilled by Jan 2022.

Those who have passed std XII in 2019 or earlier - No other eligibility conditions, go ahead and apply!

For queries: contact support@onlinedegree.iitm.ac.in
